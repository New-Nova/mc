New Nova Minecraft Server Rules

Warning Offenses - 3 Warnings → 1 Hour Mute, 3 Mutes → Temporary Ban

  • 1.1 Spamming
    Including spamming server-chat in the Discord server
  • 1.2 Using slurs
  • 1.3 Reporting messages without actual reason
    for example anything taken intentionally out of context to cause harm to another player

Warning Offenses - 3 Warnings → 1 Week Ban

  • 2.1 Claimblocking
    Only applies to active towns that haven't reached their chunk limit
    Outposts are not allowed to claimblock towns, but towns are allowed to claimblock outposts on their border
    Must be reported by the mayor of the town that is being claimblocked
    The chunks must have been claimed after the town being claimblocked was made
    If a town is found guilty of claimblocking, they must unclaim the chunks that claimblock another town or face deletion of their town
  • 2.2 Major terraforming
    Includes filling large bodies of water and creating large islands or landmasses
  • 2.3 Using X-Ray
    Using X-Ray in any circumstance is prohibited
  • 2.4 Griefing near towns
    Includes TNT, lavacasts, fire, and destroying buildings to an unrepairable degree in ruins or wilderness near a town
  • 2.5 Duping
  • 2.6 Creating or sending NSFW media

Immediate One Week Ban - 3 Bans → Permanent Ban

  • 3.1 Using cheat clients
  • 3.2 Using server exploits
  • 3.3 Lag machines or similar intents

Immediate Permanent Ban

  • 4.1 Malicious links
    Sending scam links, phishing attempts, or hack links
  • 4.2 Illegal actions
    Including but not limited to doxxing and pedophilia
